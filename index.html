Virtual Banking

In the platform’s virtual banking system, users can make purchases without immediate deductions from their available balance. Instead, their balance is only adjusted upon withdrawal, allowing them to complete transactions smoothly and view their balance in real time. Users can also send funds to an external bank, with funds deducted only when they confirm a withdrawal. This setup offers flexibility, giving users more control over their transactions while ensuring accurate, on-demand balance updates.
